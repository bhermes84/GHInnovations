About Us

The legacy continues...

In business since 1978

We are continuing the same quality of work our Opa (Glenn Hermes Sr.) provided to his clients through Glenn Hermes Illustrating in the San Antonio, Austin and New Braunfels area! We are a family owned and operated business providing the same excellent patent illustrating services our Opa gave, along with:

3D Modeling

Michael Hermes has 18 years of 3D model and graphic experience to bring your 2D drawings to life!

3D Printing

Turn your concept into a model you can show to your clients and potential investors!

Virtual Reality

We can mock up your concept in VR to show virtual demonstrations of your invention being used!
